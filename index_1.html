Cloud Lab
Automation-as-a-Service

Strateos reimagined the laboratory as a smart data generation center with the industry’s first Cloud Lab Automation-as-a-Service Platform. With high-throughput lab automation, the Strateos Platform enables scientists to design, run, and analyze experiments achieving new and faster scientific discoveries.

Use Our Cloud Lab, Or Let Us Build Yours

OUR LAB

Remote control and automated cloud labs that are accessible from a web browser enabling scientists around the world to easily access and control new technologies, drug discovery, cell and gene therapy and synthetic biology workflows. Utilizing our cloud lab as a service for drug discovery has never been more convenient.

YOUR LAB

Strateos’ smart lab facility design-build services and lab control software enables organizations to control their own scientific instruments and laboratories locally and worldwide, schedule workflows and manage their scientific data.
